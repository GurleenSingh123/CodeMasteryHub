About Us

Welcome to CodeEasy Hub, a learning platform dedicated to helping beginners and aspiring developers learn and master web development from scratch.

This website is created and maintained by Gurleen Singh. It is dedicated to providing high-quality, easy-to-understand web development tutorials for beginners. Whether you are a student, a beginner, a freelancer, or someone aiming to build a career in technology, this platform offers step-by-step guidance to support your learning journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify web development education by providing clear, structured, and practical tutorials that anyone can understand, even without prior coding experience.

We believe that learning to code should be simple and accessible, not confusing or overwhelming. That is why our tutorials focus on practical, real-world examples to make learning easier and more effective.

On this website, you will find tutorials and guides covering:

  • HTML (Structure of web pages)

We currently focus on HTML tutorials to help you build a strong foundation in web development.

Our goal is to help you not just learn theory, but also build real websites and projects.

Who This Website Is For

  • Aspiring developers preparing for careers in technology
